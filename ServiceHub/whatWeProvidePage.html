About ServiceHub

ServiceHub is a directory designed to help students and local communities discover services around Batu Pahat—then connect with providers quickly through call, WhatsApp, and maps.

What you can do
  • Search by keyword and filter by category & area.
  • Open a listing to view details, location, and quick actions.
  • Contact providers directly without extra steps.
Built for clarity
  • Clean layout focused on essential information.
  • Consistent navigation and bilingual support (EN/BM).
  • Optimized for fast browsing on student-friendly devices.
Feedback & reporting

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some common questions about how ServiceHub works and how users can use the website effectively.

How do I use ServiceHub to find a service?

You can browse the services page, search by keyword, and use category or area filters to quickly find the service you need.

Can I contact the service provider directly?

Yes. Most listings provide direct contact options such as phone call, WhatsApp, and map directions so users can connect with providers easily.

Is ServiceHub free to use?

Yes. ServiceHub is designed as a simple directory website for users to explore local services without any usage fee.

Can I submit feedback or report a listing?

Yes. Users can give reviews on service details pages and use the Contact / Report page to submit feedback, suggestions, or report listing issues.
