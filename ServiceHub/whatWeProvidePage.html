About ServiceHub

ServiceHub is a directory designed to help students and local communities discover services around Batu Pahat—then connect with providers quickly through call, WhatsApp, and maps.

What you can do
  • Search by keyword and filter by category & area.
  • Open a listing to view details, location, and quick actions.
  • Contact providers directly without extra steps.
Built for clarity
  • Clean layout focused on essential information.
  • Consistent navigation and bilingual support (EN/BM).
  • Optimized for fast browsing on student-friendly devices.
Feedback & reporting
  • Add reviews to help others compare options.
  • Report a listing easily through Contact / Report.
  • Encourages a better, safer directory experience.
